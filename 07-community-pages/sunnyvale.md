## Neighborhoods to Know

### Downtown Sunnyvale
Walkable dining and shopping along Murphy Avenue. Charming older homes and new townhome developments. Ideal for those who want an urban-village feel.

### Lakewood Village
Family-friendly neighborhood with mid-century homes, mature trees, and proximity to Lakewood Park. One of Sunnyvale's most sought-after areas.

### Ponderosa / Fair Oaks
Quiet residential streets with larger lots. Close to Fremont High School and Ponderosa Park. Popular with families looking for space.

### Birdland
Named for streets like Wren, Robin, and Oriole. Affordable entry point into Sunnyvale with strong appreciation potential. Near Raynor Park and Activity Center.

### Cherry Chase
Premium neighborhood near Cherry Chase Elementary. Well-maintained homes, wide streets, and a strong sense of community.
