## Neighborhoods to Know

### Monta Vista
Premium neighborhood adjacent to Monta Vista High School. Larger lots, hillside views, and proximity to Rancho San Antonio open space. Cupertino's most prestigious address.

### Garden Gate
Coveted for school assignments (Faria Elementary, Kennedy Middle). Quiet streets, well-maintained homes, strong community feel. One of the most competitive neighborhoods in Cupertino.

### Rancho Rinconada
Mid-century homes with large lots near Cupertino Memorial Park. Popular with families who want space. Eichler homes scattered throughout.

### Oak Valley
Newer construction with modern floor plans. Close to shopping and dining at Cupertino Village and Main Street Cupertino.

### Seven Springs
Townhome and single-family community near Stevens Creek Blvd. Good entry point into Cupertino schools at a relatively lower price point.
