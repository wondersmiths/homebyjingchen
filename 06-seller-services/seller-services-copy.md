## My Selling Process

### Step 1: Pricing Strategy

**The right price is the foundation of everything.**

I don't guess. I analyze:
- Recent comparable sales within your neighborhood (not just your zip code)
- Active and pending listings competing for the same buyers
- Price-per-square-foot trends over the past 6-12 months
- Unique features that add or reduce value
- Current buyer demand and seasonal patterns

My pricing strategy is designed to generate maximum interest and multiple offers — positioning your home at the sweet spot that attracts serious buyers while protecting your equity.

*You'll receive a detailed Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) with my pricing recommendation and the data behind it.*

---

### Step 2: Pre-Listing Preparation

**First impressions aren't just important — they're everything.**

Before your home hits the market, I coordinate:
- **Professional staging consultation** — Highlight your home's best features and create an aspirational lifestyle
- **Repairs and updates** — I'll advise on high-ROI improvements (paint, fixtures, landscaping) and skip what doesn't move the needle
- **Pre-listing inspections** — Get ahead of buyer objections by identifying and addressing issues upfront
- **Disclosure preparation** — Complete, organized disclosures build buyer confidence and reduce deal risk

I manage the vendors, timeline, and logistics — you focus on your move.

---

### Step 3: Professional Marketing

**Your home will look its best and reach the right buyers.**

My marketing package includes:
- **Professional photography** — HDR, twilight, and aerial/drone shots
- **3D virtual tour and floor plan** — So buyers can explore your home from anywhere
- **Compelling listing copy** — Written to highlight what makes your home special, not generic MLS descriptions
- **Targeted digital advertising** — Facebook, Instagram, and Google ads targeted to qualified buyers in the Bay Area and beyond
- **International exposure** — For properties that appeal to international buyers, I leverage bilingual marketing and global listing platforms
- **Agent networking** — Direct outreach to top buyer agents working your price range and neighborhood
- **Open house strategy** — Broker tour + public open houses timed for maximum traffic

---

### Step 4: Showings & Offer Management

**I manage the process so you don't have to.**

Once your home is live:
- I coordinate all showings and provide feedback from buyer agents
- I track showing activity and adjust strategy if needed
- When offers come in, I prepare a clear comparison covering price, terms, contingencies, buyer qualification, and timeline
- I advise on each offer's strengths and risks — so you make informed decisions, not emotional ones

---

### Step 5: Negotiation & Counteroffer Strategy

**This is where the right agent makes the biggest difference.**

Multiple offers? I'll help you:
- Evaluate net proceeds, not just headline price
- Identify the strongest overall offer (financing strength, contingency terms, flexibility)
- Structure counteroffers or highest-and-best calls to maximize your position
- Negotiate repair credits and appraisal gaps from a position of strength

Single offer? I negotiate with equal intensity — pushing for the best price and terms while keeping the deal together.

---

### Step 6: Closing

**I manage every detail through the finish line.**

From accepted offer to recording:
- I coordinate with the buyer's agent, escrow, and title
- I monitor all contingency timelines and deadlines
- I troubleshoot any issues — appraisal, inspection, financing — before they become problems
- I ensure a smooth final walkthrough and on-time closing

You'll know exactly where things stand at every stage. No surprises.
