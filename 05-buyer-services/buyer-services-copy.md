## My Buyer Process

### Step 1: Strategy Session

**Before we look at a single home, we build your plan.**

We'll sit down (in person or virtually) to discuss:
- Your goals, timeline, and budget
- Your must-haves vs. nice-to-haves
- Neighborhoods that match your lifestyle
- The current market conditions and what they mean for your search
- Pre-approval strategy (I'll connect you with trusted lenders if needed)

*You'll walk away with a clear, personalized roadmap — not a generic search alert.*

---

### Step 2: Targeted Home Search

**I don't send you every listing. I send you the right ones.**

Using deep local knowledge, MLS data, and off-market connections, I curate homes that match your criteria. For every property I recommend, I've already reviewed:
- Recent comparable sales
- Price-per-square-foot trends for the neighborhood
- School ratings and proximity
- Commute times to major tech campuses
- Potential red flags (permits, HOA issues, flood zones)

I also monitor pocket listings and coming-soon properties so you see opportunities before they hit the open market.

---

### Step 3: Showings & Evaluation

**Every showing is a strategic evaluation, not a casual tour.**

When we walk through a home together, I'll help you see beyond the staging:
- Is the layout functional for your family's needs?
- Are there deferred maintenance issues?
- What's the realistic value vs. the list price?
- How competitive will offers be on this property?

I'll give you honest assessments so you can make informed decisions — even if it means walking away from a home you like.

---

### Step 4: Winning Offer Strategy

**This is where Silicon Valley expertise matters most.**

Crafting a winning offer in a multi-bid situation is both art and science. My approach includes:

- **Competitive pricing analysis** — I'll identify the likely selling price range based on comps, days on market, and offer activity
- **Offer structure optimization** — Strategic use of escalation clauses, appraisal gap coverage, and flexible timelines
- **Seller-centric presentation** — Your offer tells a story. I craft terms and communication that resonate with the seller's priorities
- **Contingency strategy** — Thoughtful decisions about inspection, appraisal, and loan contingencies (I'll never ask you to waive something without explaining the risks)

My track record in winning competitive offers is one of the top reasons clients choose to work with me.

---

### Step 5: Due Diligence & Negotiation

**Once your offer is accepted, I protect your investment.**

During the contract period, I coordinate:
- Home inspection with trusted, thorough inspectors
- Review of disclosures, title reports, and HOA documents
- Appraisal process management
- Repair negotiations — I advocate firmly for credits or repairs when issues arise
- Regular check-ins with your lender to keep financing on track

If something unexpected surfaces, I'll guide you through your options with calm, clear advice.

---

### Step 6: Closing & Beyond

**I manage every detail so closing day is a celebration, not a scramble.**

From final walkthrough to signing — I ensure nothing falls through the cracks. And my service doesn't end at the closing table:
- I'll share my network of trusted contractors, designers, and handymen
- I'm available for market questions anytime
- When you're ready for your next move, I'll be here
